This is a **draft template** — it won't show on the site because `draft: true`.

To publish your first post:

1. Copy this file and rename it (the filename becomes the URL slug,
   e.g. `my-first-post.md` → `/blog/my-first-post`).
2. Edit the frontmatter above (title, description, date, tags, emoji).
3. Set `draft: false` (or remove the line).
4. Write your post here in Markdown.

Until you do, the Blog page just shows a friendly "no posts yet" message.
